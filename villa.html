05 Private bathroom

Larger, practical and easy to share

Room to refresh. Access from both sides.

The Jungle Villa includes a large private bathroom positioned behind both sleeping areas.

Two separate doors provide direct access from the king and twin zones. The bathroom follows the practical Thai wet-room style, but its larger dimensions create clear separation between the shower and toilet areas.

Hot water, a larger shower head, towels and essential toiletries are provided for a comfortable everyday routine.

Spacious Thai-style bathroom The shower is not separated by a glass enclosure.

07 The twin sleeping area

Space that adapts to the people you bring

Two more beds. One shared place to stay.

The twin area gives the Villa its flexibility without dividing the accommodation into small enclosed rooms.

The two single beds follow the same overall comfort standard as the king bed and can occasionally be joined when operationally appropriate.

Both sleeping zones remain connected to the central living room, making the Villa sociable and spacious while still giving each side its own clearly defined place.

08 Is it right for you?

A stay for people who value space

Made for guests who want room to live.

The Jungle Villa is ideal when shared time, indoor comfort and flexibility matter as much as having a peaceful place to sleep.

+ You will probably love it if

  • You are travelling as a family, group of friends or two couples.
  • You want a genuine living room, not only sleeping space.
  • You are planning a longer stay or expect to spend time indoors.
  • You value panoramic garden views and abundant natural light.
  • You are a couple who simply prefers a much more spacious accommodation.

– Consider another option if

  • You only need a comfortable base for sleeping.
  • You plan to spend almost every day away exploring Koh Chang.
  • Budget matters more than additional internal space.
  • You require separate enclosed bedrooms rather than an open-plan layout.
