05Your private bathroom

A real bathroom, exclusively yours

Full privacy. Only three steps away.

Every Yurt has its own fully enclosed private bathroom in a permanent masonry building on the same raised platform.

It is not shared, not communal and not a camping facility. From the Yurt door, you cross approximately 1.5 metres of your private deck to reach it.

Inside you will find a private toilet, hot shower, washbasin, mirror, lighting, towels and essential toiletries.

08Is it right for you?

For guests who choose experience

Made for people who want to feel the island.

Choose the Yurt when atmosphere, outdoor living and an unusual connection with nature matter more than conventional hotel-room design.

+You will probably love it if

  • You are travelling as a couple and want a memorable glamping experience.
  • You enjoy reading, relaxing or having drinks on a private deck.
  • You value jungle views, natural sounds and a feeling of immersion.
  • You appreciate minimalist interiors rather than extensive room facilities.
  • You want privacy without feeling enclosed inside a conventional building.

–Consider another option if

  • You require extremely cold air conditioning throughout the night.
  • You are very sensitive to birds, insects or other natural sounds.
  • You are uncomfortable stepping briefly onto the deck to reach the bathroom.
  • You prefer a completely conventional hotel-room experience.
  • You are travelling with children; Yurts are reserved for adults for safety.
