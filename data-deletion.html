Data Deletion Policy

Your privacy matters to us. You may request deletion of your personal information at any time.

Request Data Deletion

If you have contacted Vanara Eco Resort through our website, WhatsApp, LINE, Facebook, Instagram or any other communication channel, you may request deletion of your personal data.

Email your request to:

administrator@vanarakc.com

What to include

Please include your full name and, if applicable, your booking reference or the email address used to contact us. This helps us identify your information and process your request more quickly.

Processing Time

We normally process deletion requests within 30 days, except where we are legally required to retain certain information for accounting, legal or regulatory obligations.

Questions

If you have any questions regarding this policy or your personal information, please contact us using the email address above.
