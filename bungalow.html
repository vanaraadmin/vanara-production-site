05 Private bathroom

Simple, private and practical

Refresh in privacy. Keep things uncomplicated.

Every Jungle Bungalow includes its own private bathroom, giving you complete independence throughout your stay.

The bathroom follows the practical Thai wet-room style, with the shower and toilet sharing one easy-to-clean space. It is straightforward, functional and designed for tropical island living.

Fresh towels and essential bathroom amenities are provided, so you can arrive, settle in and begin enjoying Koh Chang.

Thai-style wet room The shower is not separated by a glass enclosure.

07 Outside your door

The room is only half the experience

Open the door. The jungle is already there.

At Vanara, the transition between accommodation and nature happens immediately.

Step outside and you are among palms, broad tropical leaves, garden paths and the everyday sounds of Koh Chang. The landscape provides natural separation between the bungalows and gives each part of the resort its own atmosphere.

This is not a manicured city hotel environment. Leaves fall, insects move through the garden and island wildlife may occasionally appear nearby. That natural presence is part of staying here.

08 Is it right for you?

A stay for independent travellers

Made for guests who came for the island.

The Jungle Bungalow works best for travellers who value privacy, atmosphere and practical comfort more than oversized interiors or resort-style excess.

+ You will probably love it if

  • You are travelling alone or as a couple.
  • You want a private room surrounded by greenery.
  • You plan to explore Koh Chang during the day.
  • You appreciate simple spaces with natural character.
  • You enjoy hearing the jungle and island wildlife nearby.

– Consider another option if

  • You need a large room for a family or group.
  • You prefer a fully enclosed luxury-hotel environment.
  • You expect a separate shower enclosure.
  • You want a minibar or extensive in-room facilities.
