04 Water within the garden

A quieter centre

Water moves gently. The atmosphere moves with it.

Water gardens bring movement, reflection and another layer of life into Vanara.

They are part of the landscape rather than a staged attraction: places passed on the way to your accommodation, noticed from nearby paths and experienced differently throughout the day.

After rain, in early light or as evening settles in, the water changes the mood of the garden without asking for attention.

08 Is Vanara right for you?

A retreat for guests who value atmosphere

Made for people who want to feel where they are.

Vanara is most rewarding when you are looking for authenticity, calm and a genuine connection with Koh Chang's tropical environment.

+ You will probably love Vanara if

  • You prefer an intimate independent resort to a large hotel complex.
  • You enjoy tropical greenery, natural sounds and real island wildlife.
  • You value atmosphere, character and human hospitality over polished luxury.
  • You want a calm base from which to explore Koh Chang.
  • You are comfortable with nature being present rather than perfectly controlled.

– Consider another stay if

  • You expect direct beachfront access from your room.
  • You want a fully serviced international luxury resort.
  • You prefer highly controlled landscaping with little visible wildlife.
  • You are looking for nightlife, entertainment or a party atmosphere onsite.
  • Steps, paths and a naturally layered garden setting do not suit your mobility needs.
